Our Services


We are a team of experienced professionals who are passionate about custom t-shirt printing. We use the latest printing technology and offer high-quality products at competitive prices. We are also committed to providing our customers with excellent customer service.

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a traditional printing method that uses a stencil to apply ink to a t-shirt. This method is ideal for creating large, detailed designs.

Direct-to-Garment Printing

Direct-to-garment printing is a newer printing method that uses inkjet technology to apply ink directly to a t-shirt. This method is ideal for creating small, intricate designs.

Heat Transfer Printing

Heat transfer printing is a method of applying a pre-printed design to a t-shirt using heat and pressure. This method is ideal for creating small, simple designs.

Sublimation Printing

Sublimation printing is a method of printing that uses heat and pressure to transfer a design onto a polyester t-shirt. This method is ideal for creating designs that are full-color and vibrant.

Embroidery

Embroidery is a method of using needle and thread to sew a design onto a t-shirt. This method is ideal for creating designs that are textured and durable.

Rhinestones

Rhinestones are small, sparkly stones that can be applied to a t-shirt to create a glamorous look.

Flocking

Flocking is a method of applying a soft, fuzzy material to a t-shirt to create a velvety texture.

Foil printing

Foil printing is a method of applying a metallic foil to a t-shirt to create a shiny, luxurious look.

Glow-in-the-Dark Printing

Glow-in-the-dark printing is a method of applying a phosphorescent material to a t-shirt that will glow in the dark.
